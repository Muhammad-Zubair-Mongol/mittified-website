# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Pakistani YouTube ecosystem audience, vlogging fans, commentators, and administrators looking for metrics, exposés, and drama indexes.

## Product Purpose
Deliver high-fidelity news, metrics analytics, and chronological drama tracking for the Pakistani YouTube creator scene.

## Brand Personality
Bold, premium, dramatic, cinematic, and authoritative. Highlights are driven by "Supercluster Yellow" (#FFD700) on deep backgrounds.

## Anti-references
- Saturated cream/sand layouts (AI defaults).
- Side-stripe borders on list cards.
- Low-contrast text on colored banners.
- Generic SaaS grid layouts that feel like admin tools instead of a media publication.

## Design Principles
1. **Cinematic authoritative visual rhythm**: Clean typography pairs with high-impact color accents to make news feel premium.
2. **Clutter-free, zero-shift media blocks**: Ads and embeds maintain explicit dimensions to guarantee visual stability.
3. **Performance-first interactions**: Quick filters and state swaps operate with smooth ease-out curves and fast edge loadtimes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG AA text contrast ratios against dark backgrounds.
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion variables.
- Anchor ads responsibly to prevent scroll-jumps on mobile screen readers.
